Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 03, 2026

Thank you for using FitHeroRPG ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

FitHeroRPG is an offline-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers. All data generated by the application is stored locally on your device.

  • Workout History: Your exercise logs (dates, repetitions, types) are stored in a local database (SQLite) on your phone.
  • Body Metrics: Your weight and height settings are stored in the app's local preferences (SharedPreferences) solely to calculate estimated calories burned.

2. Permissions and Sensors

To function correctly, the application requires access to specific hardware sensors on your device:

  • Proximity Sensor: Used exclusively to detect push-ups when the device is placed on the floor. No data from this sensor is recorded or shared.
  • Accelerometer: Used to detect squat movements. The raw sensor data is processed in real-time to count repetitions and is not saved permanently.

3. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect your data, we do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Rights (Data Deletion)

You have full control over your data. You can delete your entire workout history and personal settings at any time by using the "Reset History & Data" button located in the app's Settings menu. Uninstalling the application will also permanently remove all local data.

5. Medical Disclaimer

MFitHeroRPG is a tool for tracking physical activity and provides estimated calorie burn data. It is not a medical device. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional before beginning any new diet or exercise program.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Ideally, you should check this page periodically for any changes.
